Jacksonville Worm Farming

Welcome to Jacksonville Worm Farming!

Discover the amazing world of worm farming right here in Jacksonville, FL. Learn how to turn your kitchen waste into nutrient-rich compost for your plants, perfect for our local climate. Get started with our simple guide:

  • Step 1: Choose a suitable location for your worm farm, preferably a shady spot in your garden or balcony to protect worms from the Florida heat.
  • Step 2: Set up a worm bin or purchase a ready-made one from a local garden center or online retailer.
  • Step 3: Add bedding material such as shredded newspaper, cardboard, or coconut coir to the worm bin.
  • Step 4: Introduce composting worms (e.g., red wigglers) to the bin. You can buy them online or from a local supplier in the Jacksonville area.
  • Step 5: Start feeding your worms with kitchen scraps such as fruit and vegetable peels, coffee grounds, and crushed eggshells. Avoid feeding them meat, dairy, or oily foods.
  • Step 6: Harvest the worm castings (vermicompost) every 2-3 months and use them to fertilize your plants. They'll love it, especially in our Floridian soil!

Happy worm farming, Jacksonville!
